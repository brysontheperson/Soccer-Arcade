Arcade car soccer

Rocket League Arena

Battle a full field of bots, toggle autopilot for your car, and try to run up the score before time expires.

Controls

WASD / Arrows move, Shift boost, Space dodge, T toggles auto play.

Modes

2v2 match with three bots active at all times, including an ally bot.

Tip

Use boost on straight runs and dodge into the ball when lined up on goal.
